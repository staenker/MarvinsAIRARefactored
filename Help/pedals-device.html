When turned on, MAIRA attempts to connect to your Simagic pedals.

If no compatible device is found, MAIRA shows: [No Compatible Simagic Pedals Found]

Simagic Haptic Pedal Reactor (HPR), P-HPR GT, and P-HPR Neo reactors are supported, when connected to P500, P700, P1000, or P2000 pedal bases, or to a P2000-HCB.
