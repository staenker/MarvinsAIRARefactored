Racing Wheel

Parked Effects

Gentle low‑speed behavior: fade overall strength down, add a touch of friction, and optionally keep the wheel centered while stopped.

Strength

Scales the final output torque while stopped. At 10%, parked torque is one‑tenth of your normal strength at 0 mph, blending up to full strength by ~5 mph.

Default: 10%

Range: 0 → 100% (OFF when at 100%)

Friction

Adds a small friction torque only when stopped, which helps calm micro‑oscillation or wheel wobble in the pits. As you roll away, it blends back to your normal racing friction.

Default: OFF (0%)

Range: 0 → 100% (OFF when at 0%)

Center wheel while parked

Applies the standard wheel‑centering torque when parked (uses your global Centering Strength). It’s blended with the Center wheel while racing setting based on speed.

Default: On

Pro tip: If your wheel oscillates in the pits, increae Friction and enable Center wheel while parked. If the wheel feels too heavy while stopped, lower Strength.
