Steering Effects

Wheel Vibration Effect

Generate patterned vibration on the wheel (selecting Vibration Pattern: None disables this effect) when Understeer, Oversteer, or Seat of Pants signals are active.

Vibration Pattern

Waveform shape. Options: None, Sine Wave, Square Wave, Triangle Wave, Sawtooth Wave (In), Sawtooth Wave (Out).

Defaults: Understeer Sine Wave, Oversteer None, Seat of Pants None.

Strength

How strong the vibration can get at full signal.

Default: 10%

Range: 0 → 30%

Minimum Frequency

Frequency used as the signal just exceeds the threshold.

Default: 15 Hz

Range: 0 → 50 Hz

Maximum Frequency

Frequency used when the signal reaches 100%.

Default: 50 Hz

Range: 0 → 50 Hz

Curve

Shapes how quickly the effect ramps with the signal. Negative values front‑load the response (more feel near threshold); positive values delay it until higher signal.

Default: 25%

Range: −100 → 100%

Only Strength is interpolated with the signal. Frequency stays at Minimum Frequency until the signal reaches 100%, then switches to Maximum Frequency instantly. The Curve shapes how Strength grows, not the frequency.
