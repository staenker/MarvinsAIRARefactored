Racing Wheel

Switches

Quick on/off toggles for everyday behavior — centering, fade‑in/out, vendor LEDs, UI simplicity, and (optionally) extra headroom for strength.

Center wheel while racing

Applies your configured centering torque while racing. Cross‑fades with Center wheel while parked as speed moves through 0→5 mph.

Default: Off

Center wheel while parked

Keeps a gentle centering torque while parked (0–5 mph) to prevent wobble. Uses the same centering strength you set in Other Features → Wheel Centering Strength.

Default: On

Fade force feedback when entering or exiting car

Smoothly fades force‑feedback when entering/exiting the car, changing sessions, or when MAIRA suspends/unsuspends FFB.

Default: On

Enable Logitech RPM lights

Drives the LED RPM bar on supported Logitech wheels via the Logitech SDK. Lights scale with engine RPM reported by iRacing.

Requires a compatible Logitech wheel and the Logitech driver/runtime.

Default: On

Allow up to 200% strength (potentially dangerous)

Raises the upper limit for Strength from 100% to 200%.

High strengths can be dangerous. Use with care and keep an emergency Power button mapped.

Default: Off

Enable FFB even if iRacing simulator FFB is on

Allows MAIRA’s FFB to run even when iRacing’s own FFB is enabled. If this is Off, MAIRA will pause its output whenever iRacing’s Enable Force Feedback is on (to avoid double FFB).

Leave this Off unless you know you want both systems active (risk of doubled forces / conflicts).

Default: Off

Enable simple mode

Hides advanced controls in the UI to keep things clean.

Default: Off
