Racing Wheel

Output

Final shaping and safety controls applied after your chosen algorithm.

Minimum

Raises very small torques up to a floor (and mirrors for negative). This helps overcome static friction and makes subtle forces around center more noticeable.

Default: OFF (0%)

Range: 0 → 10% (OFF when at 0%)

Pro tip: Only use this for gear or belt driven wheels; leave this off for direct drive wheels.

Maximum

Caps the absolute torque magnitude symmetrically. Lower this to keep peaks comfortable on powerful bases.

Default: OFF (100%)

Range: 20 → 100% (OFF when at 100%)

Curve

Shapes how torque grows with input. Increasing the curve applies a non-linear response: more or less sensitivity to mid‑range cues.

Applied before soft clipping and after the algorithm.

Default: OFF (0%) (linear)

Range: -100 → 100% (OFF when set to 0%)
