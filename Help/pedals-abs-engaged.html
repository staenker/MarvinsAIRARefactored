Pedals

ABS Engaged

Distinct pulsing when the car’s ABS is active.

Frequency

The frequency to use for this effect, as a percentage between the global minimum and maximum frequency.

Default: 50%

Range: 0 → 100%

Amplitude

Overall level of the ABS pulse.

Default: 100%

Range: 0 → 100%

Fade with Brake

When on, amplitude scales with your brake input.

Default: On

ABS detection comes from the iRacing ABS-active signal telemetry.
