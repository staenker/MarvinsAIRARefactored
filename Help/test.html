Test

Play a short vibration to confirm your wheel is connected and force feedback is flowing.

Racing Wheel FFB Test Quick Action
UI Location
Racing Wheel page • Button labeled Test
Default
Action (no stored value)
What it does
Triggers a brief (~2 seconds) vibration pattern on the selected Steering Device to verify the force feedback path.

How it works

  • Press Test to set “Play test signal” internally.
  • MAIRA’s wheel engine starts a short test signal and automatically stops when it finishes.
  • The test respects your current strength/filters (so if output is limited, the test will feel gentler).
Use this after changing Steering Device, Wheel Force, or algorithm settings to validate the feel.

Related controls

  • Button mappings — Bind a physical button to trigger Test. (Setting: RacingWheelTestButtonMappings)
  • Power — If Power is Off, no FFB will be sent. Turn Power on first.

Tips & safety

  • Keep hands lightly on the wheel the first time—ensure nothing obstructs rotation.
  • If nothing happens, confirm Steering Device is selected and powered, and Power is On.
