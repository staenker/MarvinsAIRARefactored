Steering Effects

Wheel Constant Force Effect

Adds a directional constant torque to the wheel based on Understeer, Oversteer, or Seat of Pants signals.

Direction

None, Decrease Force, or Increase Force. Choosing None disables the effect.

Defaults: Understeer None, Oversteer Increase Force, Seat of Pants None.

Decrease Force blends output toward 0; Increase Force pushes in the current torque direction.

Strength

Scale of the added/removed torque at full signal.

Defaults: Understeer 10%, Oversteer 50%, Seat of Pants 50%

Range: 0 → 100%

Curve

Shapes how quickly the force builds with the signal.

Default: 0% (linear)

Range: −100 → 100%

Pro tip: Try Decrease Force for Understeer to “lighten” the wheel as grip is lost, and Increase Force for Oversteer to nudge counter‑steer reactions.
