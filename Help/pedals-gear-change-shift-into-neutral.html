Pedals

Gear Change (Shift Into Neutral)

Short tactile bump when the transmission is moved into neutral.

Frequency

The frequency to use for this effect, as a percentage between the global minimum and maximum frequency.

Default: 30%

Range: 0 → 100%

Amplitude

Strength of the bump.

Default: 70%

Range: 0 → 100%

Duration

Length of the bump in seconds.

Default: 0.05 sec

Range: 0.05 → 1 sec
