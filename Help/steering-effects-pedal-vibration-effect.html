Drive pedal haptic motors based on Understeer, Oversteer, or Seat of Pants signals.

Steering Effects page • sections Understeer — Pedal Vibration Effect, Oversteer — Pedal Vibration Effect, and Seat of Pants — Pedal Vibration Effect
What it does
Applies a vibration to your pedal haptic motors. Only Strength is interpolated with the signal. Frequency stays at Minimum until the signal reaches 100%, then switches to Maximum instantly. The Curve shapes how Strength grows, not the frequency.

Settings

  • Minimum Frequency — start point for vibration frequency.
    Range: 0 → 100%. Default: 0%.
  • Maximum Frequency — end point at full signal.
    Range: 0 → 100%. Default: 100%.
  • Curve — shapes how frequency increases with the signal.
    Range: −100 → +100%. Default: 0% (linear).

These controls show percentages in the UI. All support +/− button mappings and Context Switches.
