Racing Wheel

Protection

Automatic safeguards that temporarily reduce force after big impacts or harsh curb hits so you can keep control.

Crash Protection

When a very large g‑force is detected (e.g., from a heavy impact), MAIRA temporarily reduces the wheel’s output. During the protection window, forces are scaled down and then gradually return to normal.

G-Force — Default: 8g (Range: 2–20g; OFF at 20g)

Force Reduction — Default: 95% (Range: 0-100%; OFF at 0%)

Duration — Default: 5 sec (Range: 0–10 sec; OFF at 0 sec)

CURB PROTECTION

When a sharp suspension shock is detected (high shock velocity), MAIRA briefly reduces output to soften curb strikes or sausage kerb hits. The reduction automatically releases after the set duration.

Shock Velocity — Default: 0.5 m/s (Range: 0—1 m/s; OFF at 0 m/s)

Force Reduction — Default: 75% (Range: 0—100%; OFF at 0%)

Duration — Default: 0.1 sec (Range: 0—1 sec; OFF at 0 sec)

Pro tip: Keep an emergency shut‑off mapped — bind a button to Power so you can instantly cut FFB. Crash/Curb Wheel LFE are automatic backups; the Power button is your manual override.
