Steering Effects — Grip‑O‑Meter

A lightweight, always‑on‑top overlay that visualizes steering‑related grip and your Seat of Pants cue while you drive.

Steering Effects Overlay Grip
UI Location
Steering Effects page • section Grip-O-Meter
What it shows
  • Slip/Grip indicator — a moving dot that reflects your current combined slip level (0 → max). Further away from the center means more slip.
  • Seat of Pants indicator — a moving dot that follows your SOP effect. It can move left/right to indicate lateral direction.

Settings

  • Show Grip-O-Meter Window — show or hide the overlay window. Default: Off
  • Make the Grip-O-Meter window draggable — when enabled, you can drag the overlay by clicking it. When disabled, the window becomes click‑through so it won’t steal clicks from your sim. Default: Off
  • Grip-O-Meter Scale — resizes the overlay via a layout scale transform. Range: 50 → 200%; Default: 100%
  • Reset the Grip-O-Meter window position — moves the window back to the top‑left of your screen. The position is saved automatically whenever you move the window.
Note: The overlay will show up only when your car is on the track.

How it works

  • Always on top: The overlay is top‑most and hidden from Alt‑Tab.
  • Position memory: The window’s Position and Scale are stored in your settings and restored next launch.
  • Click‑through mode: With Make the Grip-O-Meter window draggable off, the window ignores mouse input so you can interact with the sim underneath.
