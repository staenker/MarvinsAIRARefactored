Steering Effects

Grip‑O‑Meter

A lightweight, always‑on‑top overlay that visualizes steering‑related grip and your Seat of Pants cue while you drive.

Show Grip-O-Meter Window

Show or hide the overlay window. The overlay will show up only when your car is on the track.

Default: OFF

Make the Grip-O-Meter window draggable

When enabled, you can drag the overlay by clicking it. When disabled, the window becomes click‑through so it won’t steal clicks from your sim.

Default: OFF

Grip-O-Meter Scale

Range: 50 → 200%

Default: 100%

Resizes the overlay via a layout scale transform.

Reset the Grip-O-Meter window position

Moves the window back to the top‑left of your screen. The position is saved automatically whenever you move the window.
