Racing Wheel

Other Features

Small but mighty refinements for everyday driving: base friction, centering while racing, and soft‑lock near the steering limits.

Racing Friction

Adds constant friction while racing to calm micro‑oscillations on straights. Blends with Parked Friction as you slow (0→5 mph).

Default: OFF (0%)

Range: 0 → 100% (OFF when at 0%)

Pro tip: If your wheel software already has support for friction, use that instead!

Wheel Centering Strength

Keeps a gentle centering torque while racing and/or parked (depending on switches). Near 0–5 mph it cross‑fades Center wheel while racing with Center wheel while parked so the wheel feels natural at a stop.

Default: 75%

Range: 0 → 100% (OFF when at 0%)

Soft Lock Strength

Adds a progressive resistive torque as you approach the car’s steering range limits (soft end‑stops). Helps prevent over‑rotation.

Default: 25%

Range 0 → 100% (OFF when at 0%)

Pro tip: Tackle oscillations methodically: start with a small amount of Racing Friction (5–10%).
