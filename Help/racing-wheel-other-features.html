Racing Wheel — Other Features

Small but mighty refinements for everyday driving: base friction, centering while racing, and soft‑lock near the steering limits.

Racing Wheel page • section Other Features
What this section does
Lets you add a touch of friction, keep a gentle centering torque while racing, and apply a progressive “soft lock” as you approach the steering end‑stops.

Racing Friction

  • Default: OFF (0%)
  • Range: 0–100%
  • OFF: 0% (no extra friction)
  • What it does: Adds constant friction while racing to calm micro‑oscillations on straights. Blends with Parked Friction as you slow (0→5 mph).
Pro tip: If your wheel software already has support for friction, use that instead!

Wheel Centering Strength

  • Default: 75%
  • Range: 0–100%
  • OFF: 0%
  • What it does: Keeps a gentle centering torque while racing and/or parked (depending on switches). Near 0–5 mph it cross‑fades Center wheel while racing with Center wheel while parked so the wheel feels natural at a stop.

Soft Lock Strength

  • Default: 25%
  • Range: 0–100%
  • OFF: 0% (no soft lock)
  • What it does: Adds a progressive resistive torque as you approach the car’s steering range limits (soft end‑stops). Helps prevent over‑rotation.
Pro tip: Tackle oscillations methodically: start with a small amount of Racing Friction (5–10%).
