Pedals

Wheel Spin

Cue when a driven wheel is spinning under throttle.

Frequency

The frequency to use for this effect, as a percentage between the global minimum and maximum frequency.

Default: 100%

Range: 0 → 100%

Sensitivity

Controls how likely this effect will be triggered when the engine RPM and speed of the car do not match what is predicted for the current gear.

Default: 95%

Range: 0 → 100%

Fade with Throttle

When on, amplitude scales with your throttle input.

Default: On

Due to limitations of the iRacing simulator telemetry, this is a calculated effect derived from the engine RPM, current gear, and speed of the car. This means that this effect can only estimate wheel spin for wheels connected to the engine, and only while the clutch is fully engaged.
