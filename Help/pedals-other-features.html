Other controls for the pedal effects.

Noise Damper

Some linear motors can bottom out, making a lot of noise when driven at too high amplitude at low frequencies. This setting controls the strength of a curve that reduces the amplitude at lower frequencies.

You do not need to use this for rumble-style motors (set to OFF).

Default: 10%

Range: 0 → 100% (OFF when at 0%)
