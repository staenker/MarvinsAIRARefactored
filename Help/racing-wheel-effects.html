Racing Wheel

Effects

Simple, event‑driven vibrations you can layer on top of your main FFB — handy cues without changing your core feel.

Vibrate on gear change

Triggers a brief vibration burst when a gear change is detected.

Helpful if your car’s shifts are silent or you race with low audio — gives a tactile confirmation you’ve up/down‑shifted.

This is an overlay cue; it doesn’t alter your base FFB algorithm or output curve.

Default: Off

Vibrate on ABS activation

Produces a pulsing vibration while ABS is active, helping you sense brake modulation and impending lock‑up.

Useful in cars with subtle pedal feedback or when racing on loud setups where ABS audio cues are hard to hear.

Default: Off
