What this section does
Adds lightweight, toggleable vibrations when specific events happen — currently gear changes and ABS activity. These effects are mixed after your Algorithm/Output processing.

Vibrate on gear change

  • Default: Off
  • What it does: Triggers a brief vibration burst when a gear change is detected.
  • When to use: Helpful if your car’s shifts are silent or you race with low audio — gives a tactile confirmation you’ve up/down‑shifted.
  • Note: This is an overlay cue; it doesn’t alter your base FFB algorithm or output curve.

Vibrate on ABS activation

  • Default: Off
  • What it does: Produces a pulsing vibration while ABS is active, helping you sense brake modulation and impending lock‑up.
  • When to use: Useful in cars with subtle pedal feedback or when racing on loud setups where ABS audio cues are hard to hear.
